Adidas Around the World

Before Adidas existed, Adi Dassler and his brother Rudolf Dassler ran a shoe company together called Gebrüder Dassler Schuhfabrik (Dassler Brothers Shoe Factory). However, due to personal conflicts, they split in 1948. Rudolf went on to create Puma, while Adi founded Adidas, combining his nickname Adi and part of his last name Dassler.
